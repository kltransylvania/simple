If you want to give flowers to someone, be careful of how many flowers you give. Even numbers are for the deceased, so you might want to choose an odd number of flowers.

When you're visiting someone on first of Jan, you need to take something with you when entering the house and if you are a couple it's customary that man goes first then woman, otherwise it can bring bad luck on the house.
