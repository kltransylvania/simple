Who we are

We are a manufacturing business that is focusing on handmaid home decoration items.
These items have a unique design and are focused on the natural beauty, architecture and rural uniqueness of Transylvania.
KL Transylvania focuses on creating luxurious and contemporary decorations from recycled and reclaimed wood as well as
dried natural plants and wood remains. These are combined with epoxy resin to create a unique trend that is both luxurious and contemporary.
