Few things about Transylvania

Transylvania (Romanian: Transilvania or Ardeal; Hungarian: Erdély; German: Siebenbürgen or Transsilvanien) is a historical and cultural region in Central-Eastern Europe, central Romania. Transylvania is known for the scenery of its Carpathian landscape and its rich history, coupled with its multi-cultural character.
